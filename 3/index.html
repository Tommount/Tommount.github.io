TIME


Time!

unhurried but unceasingly gone by!


Time!

you cheating runner,

people race with you desperately,

but they can never overstep the high wall at the finishing point!


Time!

you canning sophist,

people know you very well,

but none of them can really explain you when they were asked.


Time!

you ruthless death,

how many people died in regret and pain because of you,

how many people cried bitterly and solely because of you!


Time!

What a strange and fantastical creation!


Time!

You lovely angel,

you make new life born,

you make the world become full of vigour and youthfulness!


Time!

You fair referee,

your grace to everyone is the same,

but you can make idler achieve nothing.


Time!

You naughty fairy,

You are everywhere,

you’re in charge of everything,

but you always play hide and seek with us,

invisible and elusive.


Time!

Unhurried but unceasingly gone by!
