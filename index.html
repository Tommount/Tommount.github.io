Today I saw a video on youtube, about Bill Gates talking about Ebola and “we are not ready for the next epidemic” at TED, in 2015.

He listed the things that are missing:

  1. Surveillance and data
  2. Personnel
  3. Treatment

And he said “The fact these elements are missing is a GLOBAL FAILURE”

He listed what we need:

  1. Strong health systems
  2. Medical reserve crops

It lets me think of the 2019-Con virus now spreading in China, Bill Gates was right, we are not ready, if we are better prepared, maybe the situation won’t be so serious.
